What We Do

Detail Survey

The initial survey for the planning and design process, whether it be for building or subdivision purposes. This survey locates all features, levels and contours over the land whilst showing subject boundaries and applicable easements.

Select Service
Set Out Survey

During the construction phase it is important to have your dream new build, alteration or addition, pool or carport set out in the correct position. Placing survey marks in relation to these structures allows the builder to ensure they’re building as per the architectural plans.

Select Service
Identification Survey

The final stage of the process is the final identification survey and accompanying report. Showing the location of structures in relation to the boundary, the location of any encroachments or easements onto adjoining properties, the final floor levels and ridge levels.

Boundary Survey

Boundary definition surveys including placing physical marks on corners and lines for fencing purposes.
